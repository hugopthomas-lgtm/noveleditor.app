Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI model powers Novel Editor?

Novel Editor is powered by Claude, Anthropic's AI. It excels at creative writing, understanding nuance, and matching your unique style.

Can I try it before paying?

Yes! The free tier includes 10 AI suggestions per day. You can try every feature before deciding to upgrade.

What languages are supported?

English, French, Spanish, Italian, German, and Portuguese — for both the interface and the AI's ability to work with your manuscript.

Will the AI change my writing style?

Never. Novel Editor learns your unique voice and all suggestions respect your style. It helps you find your voice, not replace it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime from your billing portal. You keep Pro access until the end of your current month. After that, AI features revert to 10/day.
